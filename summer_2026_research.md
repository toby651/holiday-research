## Recommended Regions (Ranked)

### 1. PUGLIA (Salento) - Best Overall Pick

**Why:** Best large villa availability, excellent food, flat/easy terrain for kids, good beaches, best value.

**Beaches:** Pescoluse ("Maldives of Salento") - white sand, crystal clear, shallow water. Also Punta Prosciutto, Torre dell'Orso, Porto Cesareo.

**Airport:** Brindisi (BDS) - 40-80 min drive to Salento coast depending on exact location.

**Flights:**
| Airport | Airline | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Stansted | Ryanair | ~3h, multiple times/week, ~£100-170 return |
| Stansted | Ryanair | Also flies to Brindisi 1x/week |
| Bristol | easyJet/Ryanair | New Bari route for summer 2026! ~£135 return |

**Accommodation highlights:**
- Masseria del Vigneto - 10 bedrooms, pool, tennis court, resident chef, 20 min to beaches
- Masseria Giardini - 10 bedrooms, pool, accommodates 20+ guests
- Apulia Rentals has properties sleeping up to 24 guests
- Many restored masserias (farmhouses) with pools available

**Price range:** €5,000-€10,000/week for large properties (some luxury ones go higher)

**Pros:**
- Best selection of large group villas (masserias)
- Flat terrain - easy with buggies/young kids
- Outstanding food region
- Good value compared to Sardinia
- Cultural interest (Lecce, Ostuni, Alberobello trulli)

**Cons:**
- Beaches very good but not quite Sardinia-level
- Brindisi has fewer direct UK flights than other Italian airports
- 40-80 min airport transfer

---

### 2. SARDINIA (South-East Coast: Costa Rei / Villasimius) - Best Beaches

**Why:** The best white sand beaches and clearest water in the Mediterranean. Shallow water perfect for young kids.

**Beaches:** Porto Giunco, Cala Sinzias, Costa Rei - genuinely Caribbean-quality white/pink sand, turquoise shallow water.

**Airport:** Cagliari (CAG) - 45-60 min drive.

**Flights:**
| Airport | Airline | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Stansted | Ryanair | Seasonal, BUT late departures (17:30-19:00, arriving ~22:00) |
| Bristol | None direct | No direct flights to Cagliari |
| Luton | None direct | No direct flights to Cagliari |

**Alternative: Fly to Olbia (OLB) instead** - much better connected:
| Airport | Airline | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Bristol | easyJet | Seasonal, ~£162 return, reasonable times |
| Luton | easyJet | ~£111 return |
| Stansted | Ryanair/Jet2/BA | Best connected, ~£80-150 return |

> **Problem:** Olbia is in the NORTH of Sardinia. The best white sand beaches (Costa Rei/Villasimius) are in the SOUTH near Cagliari. Olbia to Costa Rei = 3+ hours drive. This makes Olbia impractical for southern beaches.

**Accommodation:** Fewer 10+ bedroom mega-villas than Puglia. More common to find 4-6 bedroom villas. May need 2-3 adjacent properties.

**Price range:** €6,000-€12,000/week for large properties

**Pros:**
- Absolute best beaches in Italy
- Shallow water perfect for toddlers
- Well-established tourist infrastructure

**Cons:**
- Flight access to south coast (Cagliari) is poor from your airports - only Stansted/Ryanair with late departure times
- Olbia has great flights but is 3+ hours from the best beaches
- More expensive than Puglia
- Fewer large group villas available
- Hillier terrain

---

### 3. SARDINIA (North-West: Alghero / Stintino) - Compromise Option

**Why:** La Pelosa beach at Stintino is arguably Italy's most famous white sand beach. Alghero airport is very close.

**Beaches:** La Pelosa - ankle-deep turquoise water, white sand. Maria Pia near Alghero also excellent.

**Airport:** Alghero (AHO) - only 10-30 min to Alghero beaches, 40-60 min to Stintino.

**Flights:**
| Airport | Airline | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Stansted | Ryanair | Seasonal, good morning and afternoon options |
| Bristol | None direct | - |
| Luton | None direct | - |

> Only Stansted has direct flights to Alghero.

**Cons:**
- La Pelosa now has daily visitor caps in summer - need to book/reserve
- Only Stansted flights
- Fewer large villas than Puglia

---

### 4. SICILY (South-East: Syracuse / Ragusa area) - Best Value

**Why:** Cheapest option, great cultural interest, good beaches, well-connected airports.

**Beaches:** Fontane Bianche (white sand, clear water), Vendicari Nature Reserve, San Lorenzo, Calamosche.

**Airport:** Catania (CTA) - 40-90 min drive.

**Flights:**
| Airport | Airline | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Bristol | easyJet | Seasonal, ~£190 return |
| Luton | easyJet/Ryanair | ~£193 return, multiple options |
| Stansted | Ryanair/Jet2 | Good frequency, from £80 return |

**Accommodation:** Good villa stock in Val di Noto area. One example: 10-bed villa in Scordia, €8,232/week.

**Pros:**
- Best flight connections from ALL THREE airports
- Most affordable accommodation
- Rich culture (baroque towns, food, Etna)
- Good beaches

**Cons:**
- Very hot in late July (35-40C)
- Beaches good but not Sardinia-level for white sand
- Longer airport transfer for the best beaches

---

### 5. CALABRIA (Tropea / Capo Vaticano) - Budget Pick

**Why:** Cheapest of all, stunning beaches, undiscovered feel.

**Beaches:** Tropea and Capo Vaticano - white sand, strikingly clear water.

**Airport:** Lamezia Terme (SUF) - 50-70 min drive.

**Flights:**
| Airport | Airline | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Stansted | Ryanair | Seasonal, reasonable times |
| Bristol | None direct | - |
| Luton | None direct | - |

> Only Stansted has direct flights.

**Cons:**
- Only accessible direct from Stansted
- Some beaches require steep staircase access (hard with buggy/young kids)
- Less developed tourism infrastructure
- Fewer large villas available

## SUMMARY & RECOMMENDATIONS

### Best overall match for our group:

**If budget allows (EUR 15,000-25,000/week range):**
- **Masseria Pistola** - 11 bedrooms, 22 guests, chef included, tennis, stunning grounds. But 60+ min to Pescoluse.
- **Masseria Ettore** - 9 bedrooms but 25 guests with kids' beds, full staff. Close to Lecce/Salento beaches.

**Best value options (under EUR 10,000/week):**
- **Palazzo Corte dei Granai** - EUR ~5,400/week, 16 rooms, Maglie (central Salento). B&B-style but could book exclusively.
- **Masseria Lamagrotte** - EUR ~6,500/week, 12 rooms, near Ostuni coast (800m to beach).
- **La Residenza Corte Dei Messapi** - EUR 5,550/week, 8 beds, Ostuni (would need supplement).

**Best for beaches (Pescoluse "Maldives of Salento"):**
- **Masseria Palaci** - Only 7 min to Pescoluse. 9 beds/18 guests. Cook included.

**Best for table tennis:**
- **Villa Elia** - Table tennis + pool table + tennis court. Gallipoli area.
- **Masseria Mangiamuso** - Table tennis + gym. Ostuni area.
- **Villa Manfredi** - Table tennis + massive pool + spa. EUR 70,000/week (way over budget).

### Two-villa strategy (recommended):
Given our budget of approx EUR 5,000-8,000 for accommodation, the most realistic approach is two adjacent or nearby villas in the same area. For example:
- Two 5-6 bedroom villas near Gallipoli/Ugento/Salve area
- Combined cost: EUR 3,000-5,000 each = EUR 6,000-10,000 total
- Look on VRBO, Airbnb, or local agencies for paired properties
