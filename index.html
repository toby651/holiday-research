Recommendation

Puglia,
Salento

Southern Italy's best-kept secret for families

🌊 Pescoluse Beach

"The Maldives of Salento" — white sand, crystal clear shallow water, perfect for babies and toddlers. Punta Prosciutto and Porto Cesareo also stunning.

🏠 Masserias

Puglia has Italy's best stock of large converted farmhouses with 10+ bedrooms, pools, and grounds. Purpose-built for big group holidays.

🍝 The Food

Arguably Italy's best food region. Fresh pasta, burrata, seafood, olive oil. Kid-friendly menus everywhere. Cheap eating out.

👶 Flat Terrain

Unlike hilly Sardinia or Sicily, Puglia is flat. Easy with buggies, easy to cycle, easy for toddlers. Less stressful for everyone.

✈️ Direct Flights

Bristol to Bari is a brand new summer 2026 route. London families can fly Stansted to Bari or Brindisi. ~3 hours, daytime flights.

🏛️ Day Trips

Lecce's baroque architecture, Alberobello's trulli houses, the white city of Ostuni. Plenty to do if anyone wants a break from the pool.

Once we've got a property and flights that work, collect deposits and lock it in. Suggest £300 per family to secure everything. Book ASAP — 10+ bed villas in peak July go fast.
